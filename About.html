About Us:

Hey There,


Welcome to COMMONVOICE community. This website is created by ASHWANI SHARMA. It was created on June 1, 2022, and is being continued till this day. The main motive to create this platform is to help the citizens of India to share their problem with the world and the second motive was to lessen the work of news reporters and news channels.

We are here to provide you the way to connect and show your problems to the government and the world, and also we try to help those who need the help.

By uploading a photo or a video here, you can help us and help yourself too. For your efforts in doing all this, we also provide a reward for your work. For more information, read our Terms and Conditions.

Hello Guys,


Welcome to COMMONVOICE community. This website is created by me as you have read above. I was born on January 8, 2004, in a middle-class family but I always wanted to create something. For that, I studied so hard, and one day I got the chance to help the society with my work.

The story behind the creation of this website was when I was in college. One day I saw a news journalist working so hard to cover an accident news, but there was too much crowd there, and there I saw an opportunity like a bright star at night. At that place, while people were helping the man that was caught in the accident, some were taking photos and creating videos, and next I also saw those photos on social media which gave me motivation to create this platform for people.

From that, I am working my foot off to create this platform. It took almost 2 years to complete this project, and now you are seeing my masterpiece.

My Contribution:

  • First, I collect your uploaded image or video sent to me from different sources like uploaded on this website, from WhatsApp, from Instagram, email, and Gmail.
  • I then analyze the image or video to be relevant and covering our terms and conditions strictly.
  • Then I present your image or video to different channels for their opinion on whether it is needed or not.
  • After approval from all our sources, I upload the image or video on my website.
  • After doing all the paperwork, I then send the person who sent the image or video an email as a thanks for contributing his work for the society and reward him or her the prize money he or she deserves.
