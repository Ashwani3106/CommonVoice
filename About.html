About Us:

Hey There,


                                     Welcome to COMMONVOICE community. This website is created by ASHWANI SHARMA. It was created on June 1,2022 and is being continued till this day. The main motive to create this platform is to help the citizens of India to share their problem to the world and the second motive was to less the work of news reporters and news channel.

We are here to provide you the way to connect and show your problems to government and world and also we try to help them who needs the help.

The story behind the creation of this website was when i was in college. One day I saw a news journalist working so hard to cover a accident news but there was too much crowd there and there I saw a opportunity like a bright star at night.
